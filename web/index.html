The Vault
The Best Home Backup System For Linux
With the Vault, you can be sure your data is safely stored away - to your backup drive, to your NAS, to your cloud storage, anywhere. In the event of a disaster, the Vault will recover all your stuff.
Highlights
  • Free open-source software!
  • A simple, intuitive interface
  • Supports cloud providers, including Dropbox and Amazon S3
  • Encryption built in out of the box
  • A comprehensive disaster recovery system
  • Automatic storage management
